Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI Trip Planner help travel agents? ▶
Our platform automates itinerary creation by analyzing traveler travel preferences, budget, and real-time availability of flights, hotels, and activities. It reduces manual work, speeds up response time, and helps travel agents close deals faster.
How does RouteMaestro ensure itinerary accuracy? ▶
The platform integrates with global suppliers to fetch real-time pricing, availability, and updated travel information. It also cross-checks routes, accommodations, and activities for feasibility and accuracy.
Can I modify the AI-generated itinerary before sharing it with my clients? ▶
Absolutely! The AI provides a well-structured itinerary, but you have full control to edit destinations, accommodations, activities, and transport options before sending it to your clients.
Does the AI provide recommendations based on customer preferences? ▶
Yes, our platform suggests hotels, activities, and transport options based on your customer travel preferences, past bookings, and trending travel destinations.
How do I get started with RouteMaestro? ▶
Simply click on "Schedule a Demo", choose your preferred date and time, and our team will reach out to you.
